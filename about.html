ABOUT

The Lighthouse book club was create to inspirng meaningful discussions while socializing and making new friends.
As a club we've read fiction books every month since October 2019. We aim to help people to read more and connect with all kinds of curious and interesting people. We cover a new theme each month with three short-listed books for members to vote on.

FAQ

What Is a Book Club?

A book club is a reading group, usually consisting of a number of people who read and talk about books based on a topic or an agreed-upon reading list.

Can anyone join it?

Of course! If you are a love reader or just want to develop a good habit, that is the place for you. We love to hear people's perspectives about the stories we read and the world we live in, so do not be shy!

Are the discussion virtual or in-person?

Both and you can choose whatever suits you better. The details regarding the virtual meet up are always available on our newsletter.

Can I suggest a place for the next meet up?

Definitely! We are all about discovering new places and new food, so we love to hear about hidden gems around the Dublin area. Just send us an email or a message through our social media!

Can I suggest books?

Yes, please do! We are always open to suggestions, especially when it comes to what we read next.
