ABOUT

The Lighthouse book club was create to inspirng meaningful discussions while socializing and making new friends. As a club we've read a non-fiction book every month since November 2019. We aim to help people to read more and connect with all kinds of curious and interesting people. We cover a new theme each month with three short-listed books for members to vote on. Members of the community recieve a bi-weekly newsletter with all the updates and news related to the club. Because of the pandemic many of our first meetup were online via Zoom, but with things getting better in-person meet up started to happen and so far it has been amazing. We are always open to suggestions and feedback.
