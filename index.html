ABOUT

What Is a Book Club?

It is simply a reading group, which is consisted of a number of people who read the same books based on a topic or an agreed-upon reading list, and get together to talk about it. Usually book clubs choose a specific book to read and discuss at the same time.

How does it work?

Every 1st of the month, you will receive our newsletter in which will be listed three books, you can vote and help us to pick one. After it, you just have to dig into the story and come meet us to talk about it on the last Saturday of the month.

How do I join?

That is super simple! All you have to do it register yourself. After registering, you wil receive an emai with extra informations. You can also choose to receive our bi-weekly newsletter with all the updates and information regarding the book club.

When does it happen?

At the end of the month, you will be invited to our get together to discuss the book. Usually it happens on the last Saturday of the month. Each meet up is held in a different location, so if you know any hidden gens, please let us know!

REASONS TO JOIN A BOOK CLUB

Food for the brain

Reading is a very good exercise for the brain. Many studies through the years have shown that reading is good for strengthening both the physical structure of the brain and improving your social soft. skills, like intelligence and empathy.

Book clubs are fun!

A book club can help you meet new people and make new friends, all in a relaxed atmosphere. atmosphere. No matter how serious your book discussions are, just getting together and chatting on a regular basis can be fun!

A break from the madnest of the day to day

Reading a book is a great way to escape into a different reality, which can be very inspiring and relaxing, especially when so much is going on around you. When the world around us become overwhelming, we all need a quick escape.

Helps you to create a health habit

Signing up for a club gives you a dedicated reason to read on a more regular basis. With a little friendly pressure from other members of the group, you are more likely to actually finish the books you do start reading!

It gives you a different perspective

While hearing other readers’ perspectives on the same story, it is an interesting way to see the same book through someone else's eyes and it might even change your own ideas about what a book means.

You Discover and Enjoy New Food

The monthly meetings are held in different places everytime, which gives you the opportunity to discover new places around the city. Enjoy new food and have a glass of wine or a cup of coffee, while also meeting new people.
