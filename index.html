REASONS TO JOIN A BOOK CLUB

Food for the brain

Reading is a very good exercise for the brain. Many studies through the years have shown that reading is good for strengthening both the physical structure of the brain and improving your social soft. skills, like intelligence and empathy.

Book clubs are fun!

A book club can help you meet new people and make new friends, all in a relaxed atmosphere. No matter how serious your book discussions are, just getting together and chatting on a regular basis can be fun!

Create a health habit

Signing up for a club gives you a dedicated reason to read on a more regular basis. With a little friendly pressure from other members of the group, you are more likely to actually finish the books you do start reading!

Different perspective

While hearing other readers’ perspectives on the same story and reflecting on it, it is an interesting way to see the same book through someone else's eyes and it might even change your own ideas about what a book means.
