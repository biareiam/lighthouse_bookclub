REASONS TO JOIN A BOOK CLUB

Food for the brain

Reading is a very good exercise for the brain. Many studies through the years have shown that reading is good for strengthening both the physical structure of the brain and improving your social soft. skills, like intelligence and empathy.

Book clubs are fun!

A book club can help you meet new people and make new friends, all in a relaxed atmosphere. No matter how serious your book discussions are, just getting together and chatting on a regular basis can be fun!

Create a health habit

Signing up for a club gives you a dedicated reason to read on a more regular basis. With a little friendly pressure from other members of the group, you are more likely to actually finish the books you do start reading!

Different perspective

While hearing other readersâ€™ perspectives on the same story and reflecting on it, it is an interesting way to see the same book through someone else's eyes and it might even change your own ideas about what a book means.

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1

Vote

Once you join the club, you will be give the power to vote and choose for 1of 3 nonfiction books shortlisted by the book ambassadors. Everyone will read the winner.

Step 2

Read

Every month members vote on a shortlist of 3 inspiring fiction books on a theme. After that you just have to dig into it. We will be checking with you weekly to see howis it going.

Step 3

Meet

In the end of the month, you will be invited to our in-person and virtual meetups to discuss the book. We always try to choose a new place for our get together, so if you know any hidden gen, let's us know!
